Evidence for better policy

Nepal’s historic transition to federalism has surged the demand for more and better data. There is a need to transform existing data into actionable insights and policy outcomes that improve people’s lives, and hold others accountable. To achieve this, Nepalis – across all the sectors – need to access and use timely and actionable data to make decisions, enhance public accountability, identify risks and opportunities, track progress, and more. Bearing this in mind, the World Bank is working to propel the data sharing and using capacity of wider population, and in the end, optimize the value of data.
