Measuring inequality of Access modeling

This paper provides descriptive analyses of accessibility trends to services at national, provincial, municipal, and geographic scales and suggests research possibilities unlocked by a general purpose model.

Nepal Development Update

Nepal Development Update is World Bank Nepal's biannual publication that reports on key economic developments over the preceding months and examines topics of particular policy significance. The special focus for December 2019 update, was on "Envisioning a future data ecosystem in a federal Nepal"

Open Data for Business Report

Open Data for Business report presents learnings from our exploratory learning exercise to assess the use of data in banking, education and media sectors in Nepal. This report is intended to help inform a long-term strategy to increase the business use of government data in Nepal.

DSET (Data Survey Evaluation Tool)

Open Data Watch in partnership with the World Bank evaluated 22 publicly available data portals in Nepal using its Data Site Evaluation Toolkit.
