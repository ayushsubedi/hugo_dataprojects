Provincial SDG Dashboard

This portal is a joint product of the National Planning Commission, Central Bureau of Statistics and the World Bank. It indicates the province level updates on the Sustainable Development Goals.

Geo Node

GeoNode, a web-based, open source software that enables users to access, share, and visualize geospatial data. It aims to solve to a set of common, overlapping problems shared by a number of different organizations.

Data in News

The OD4B team developed an indicator model to assess the use and engagement of statistics in Nepali news portals along with an accompanying dashboard visualizes the results of the analysis. The dashboard will keep track of the use of data and statistics in six news portals, as it automatically adds more articles to its analysis dataset on a daily basis.
